### That's It For The Year And The Masters!
I know I say this in just about all of my posts but this year especially trimester 03 has gone by so fast! 

**For Applied Machine Learning**

This unfortunatley didn't turn out, so my final model with the best performance had a R^2 value of **0.09**. I was a little surprised but was able to find a scientific cause and explain this in my reports resulting in some good grades!
